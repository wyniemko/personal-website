Businesses should often revamp their plans to drive growth & hold accountability. There are three key objectives to keep fun mind when developing a new SaaS strategic plan:

1. Use data to find Chokepoints within the flywheel
2. Strategic Design: identify core, and plot expansion
3 Define & Align around the Objectives & Key Results (OKRs) 

So you want to sell your product. It’s time to develop a sales strategy. Answer these three questions as you beginning building out how you want to convert customers, into revenue:

Why You?
Build trust/ connection
Make them feel understood
Highlight the problem

Why Us?
Social Proof (customers, logos)
Trusted Data (macro-tends)
Unique Point-of-view (our approach) 

Why Now?
What you will achieve with us (results)
What is at stake if you don’t act now (more risk, lose $)
