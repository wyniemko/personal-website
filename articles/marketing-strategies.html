Marketing is simply: more people knowing about you today than yesterday.

Great Marketing is:
Specific
Polarizing
Consistent

Customers are using the “product” so that they can “do this.”

Marketing isn’t selling an improvement; it’s about selling a transformation. A new way to:
- Offer the Fastest/ Cheapest / Quickest/ Best/ most premium product
- Shifting the traditional mindset/ approach

Outbound

Co-Marketing
