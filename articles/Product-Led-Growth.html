Product-Led Growth (PLG) is where business rely on product usage as the primary driver of acquisition, conversion and expansion. Want to pursue or experiment with a PLG strategy? Follow the criteria below:

1. Your user needs an early A-Ha moment with the product, because in PLG, there is need for a sales team!

2. Run many experiments per week with marketing + product + sales (white glove w/ accounts >20) to see what is working. A/B testing and user interviews are essential in this stage.

3. Up-sell + Cross-sale + Expand! Try to increase your deal size to see if PLG is effective.
