Should you choose a marketing focused or sales focused strategy?

Marketing is bottom-up (<$10k/year), start with trials and eventual salesperson

Sales is top-down. approach customer to buy (transformation, faster growth, low churn)

- Sales Development Representatives (CAC)
- Inside Sales (<$100k/year)
- Hybrid ($250k/year)
- Enterprise/ Field team (>$500k/year)

Developing a Sales Structure:
- Commission Structure: 20% commission on every $1
- Monthly with Kickers (Stacking performance)
- Sales Contracts, Script, Process

Finding synergy between product, market, go-to-market strategy:
- Authority / Expert in the problem (books, talking, learning)
- Show findings with others (conferences, blogs, webinar, tweets, LinkedIn)
- Make a Manifesto to find a core audience (differentiated, pipeline of ready to buy)

Three Key Sales Actions:
- Sales Deck (why now, why you, what macro problem are you fixing)
- Strategic Narrative (manifesto)
- Developing leads partnering with marketing (leads  Marketing qualified lead / Sales qualified lead  Account Based Marketing Strategy)
