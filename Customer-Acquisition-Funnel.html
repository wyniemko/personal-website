There are numerous approaches to customer acquisition in B2B SaaS. Some popular strategies include Account-Based Marketing (ABM) where businesses must align across Sales and Marketing to deliver a great customer experience and close more deals. Another is Product-Led Growth (PLG) where business rely on product usage as the primary driver of acquisition, conversion and expansion. 

Another few ways of acquiring customers is through outbound marketing (costly and low ROI) and digital ads (low conversion, low ROI).

Some say the sales funnel is dead. However, it is still one of the most effective ways to acquire customers. Here are a few tried and tested customer funnels for your business:

Funnel #1
1. Develop and share your Manifesto
2. Sales Conversation/ Demo (Unit Economics > $20K ARR)
3. Revenue

Funnel #2
1. Develop and share your  Manifesto
2. Free Trial ($9/month up to $20/m)
3. Revenue

Funnel #3
1. Promote Free Trial
2. Develop and share your Manifesto with focus on onboarding and outbound marketing
3. Revenue

The sales funnel is rooted in three key ideas: customer Awareness, Consideration, then Conversion. When building you’re funnel, make sure you have a strategy, but also able to execute. Don’t be afraid to experiment and see what works for your business.
