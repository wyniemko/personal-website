Cold Calling is easily dismissed as irrelevant to most, yet for B2B sales, it drives the bulk of sales and growth for many companies. These companies use software such as ZoomInfo to directly reach B2B buyers where it matters most (on their cell phones). And following a few simple strategies, you can also sell more with less work. 

Treat selling like its you DUTY. You have a responsibility to sell B2B buyers this software to drive value. Maybe it's time, money, but it's always driving a business transformation. 

No gimmicks. Be honest with the buyer, and try to mirror match them, talking in a similar way, tone, to effectively communicate and capture their attention.

Have a strong opening line. Studies show that if you acknowledge your role in sales, and ask for 27 seconds of their time, you will likely be able to capture their attention. Use this time to pitch the macro problem, your unique solution and value prop.
