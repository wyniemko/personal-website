Jim Collins developed an Ideal Customer Flywheel, that all businesses should follow in his book, “Good to Great.” He talks about how businesses should identify the key components and optimize them that will speed up and increase your flywheel that leads to more customers converting to revenue.

We identify the main parts of the flywheel below:

1. Acquisition (user)
3. Activation (meaningful steps to move forward)
3. Revenue (pay for the product)
4. Retention (low churn, keep using)
5. Referral (word of mouth, referral, invitation, share)
